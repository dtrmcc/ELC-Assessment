Ecological Languaging Competency Assessment

Research Team

We are a distinguished group of scholars from leading universities worldwide, dedicated to advancing the understanding and assessment of ecological languaging competencies in multilingual and multicultural contexts.

Our research bridges the gap between traditional language assessment and the dynamic, interconnected nature of real-world communication, fostering sustainable approaches to language education and evaluation.

Our Mission

Ecological Approach

We examine language as part of complex, interconnected ecosystems that include social, cultural, and environmental factors, moving beyond traditional isolated assessment methods.

Competency Innovation

Developing new frameworks for understanding and measuring languaging competencies that reflect real-world communication needs and sustainable practices.

Global Collaboration

Bringing together expertise from diverse institutions to create comprehensive, culturally responsive assessment tools and methodologies.

Research Areas

Ecological Languaging Competency Framework

We are developing a novel theoretical framework that understands and assesses language competency within broader ecosystems. This framework considers the complex interactions of social, cultural, cognitive, and environmental factors in language use.

Multimodal Assessment Tools

Our team is designing innovative assessment tools that evaluate not only traditional language skills but also 21st-century core competencies such as digital literacy, intercultural communication skills, and sustainability awareness.

Cross-cultural Language Practices

We conduct in-depth research on language practices across different cultural contexts, exploring how to maintain linguistic diversity while promoting effective cross-cultural communication in the era of globalization.

Technology-Enhanced Assessment

Utilizing artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, we are developing more precise, personalized language assessment systems that can adapt in real-time to learners' needs and progress.
